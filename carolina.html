About University of South Carolina

South Carolina is a highly rated public university located in Columbia, South Carolina. It is a large institution with an enrollment of 26,174 undergraduate students. Admissions is somewhat competitive as the South Carolina acceptance rate is 68%. Popular majors include Finance, Public Health, and Marketing. Graduating 78% of students, South Carolina alumni go on to earn a starting salary of $38,000.
