As a junior in high school there is tons of pressure on you to find the right college. The college you choose will ultimately set up the rest of your life and what you do after college. With so many options it can be overwhelming. I created this website to help myself weigh the pros and cons of my top colleges.

My requirements

  • Location and Weather (Florida, Georgia, South Carolina, etc.)
  • Strong buisness program
  • Greek Life
  • Sporting events to watch (school spirt/pride)
  • Good living accomidations (dorms/apartments)
  • Good Professors
